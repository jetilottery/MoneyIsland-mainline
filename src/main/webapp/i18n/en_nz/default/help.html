Money Island

Shiver me timbers! Welcome aboard Money Island where chests full of treasures await! Select chests to reveal prizes or Bonus symbols – reveal a prize to win instantly! Unlock bonuses by collecting symbols for more bountiful booty: reign victorious in the Cannon Bonus to collect prizes and multipliers or set off sail in the Island Bonus for a rewarding voyage with five islands and their treasure!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Select eight out of sixteen chests to reveal prize amounts or Bonus symbols. Reveal a prize to win that amount instantly!

Cannon Bonus

Reveal three Cannon symbols to trigger the Cannon Bonus. Select a ship to reveal either a prize amount, a multiplier, or a Collect symbol. The prize amounts are totaled at the end of the bonus and multiplied by the final multiplier value. Reveal a Collect symbol to end the bonus.

Island Bonus

Reveal three Ship symbols to trigger the Island Bonus. There are five treasure maps in the bonus, each with increasing prize values. Select two of the nine map locations to reveal coin amounts. If the number of coins meets the Coins to Unlock Treasure amount for that island, the ship advances to the next island. Each island requires an increasing number of coins to advance and is dependant on the prize offered. Continue advancing to the next island until the required number of coins for the current island is not collected, or the fifth island’s prize is awarded.

Only the highest prize from the last island completed is awarded.

The prize and coins threshold associated with each island can differ between bonus games.

Coins are collected cumulatively through the bonus.

AUTO REVEAL

Press AUTO REVEAL to play each part of the game to completion automatically. If a Bonus is triggered, the Bonus game plays after all selections have been made and any prizes have been revealed.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values. Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
